Soccer_Broker

Predicting market value of soccer players

In a no-liquid market as that one of soccer athletes, it is difficult to give a player a price based on his skills and performance. Soccer_Broker is a tool that address the issue by estimating the market value of a player based on his characteristics and most recent performances. In this way, Soccer Broker offers clubs a tool that help forecast return on their investment and make more strategic negotiation and budget decisions
